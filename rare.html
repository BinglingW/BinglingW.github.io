Project Overview

46% of homeowners say they have given serious thought to adding solar panels to their home in the past year, but only 6% of homeowners in the US have already installed solar (Pew Research Center, 2019). The gap between solar consideration and solar adoption is substantial.

In this project, I worked with 3 colleagues to understand why people aren't switching to solar and design a green benefit package to encourage employees at Fortune 500 companies to adopt solar.
